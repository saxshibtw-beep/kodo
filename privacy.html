What data we collect

Your email is used to notify you about drops, order updates, and occasional brand transmissions. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing. Ever.

We use minimal cookies — only what's needed for the site to function and for basic analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.
Your rights
You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time via the link in any email we send. To request deletion of your data, email orders@wearkodo.com and we'll process it within 30 days.

We use Shopify for our store, Formspree for form submissions, and Railway for hosting. Each of these has their own privacy policies. We choose services with strong privacy standards.
